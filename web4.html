Web 4

Web4 (or Web 4.0) is a hypothetical next-generation internet stage often described as the "Intelligent Web" or "Symbiotic Web". While Web3 focuses on decentralization and ownership, Web4 aims to integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), and virtual environments to create a deeply personalized, predictive, and autonomous digital ecosystem.
