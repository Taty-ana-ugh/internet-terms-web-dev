Web 1

Web1 (Web 1.0) is the first iteration of the internet, lasting roughly from 1991 to 2004, defined as a "read-only" web. It consisted of static, informational pages owned by organizations, where users were passive consumers with no interactive features like comments or likes. Technologies used were basic HTML, GIFs, and frames.
