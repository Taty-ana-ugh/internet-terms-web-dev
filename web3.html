Web 3

Web3 is the emerging third generation of the internet, characterized as a decentralized, open, and user-owned web built on blockchain technology. It aims to shift control of data from major corporations (like Google or Facebook) to individual users, allowing them to own their digital assets, identity, and data, while facilitating "trustless" and "permissionless" interactions.
